Entry point for the standalone linear-Compton benchmark executable.

The executable is used in two ways:

  • directly from the command line to regenerate CSV benchmark references,
  • indirectly from TestLinearComptonSpectrum, where it acts as a small regression-data generator for the finite-beam cases.

Single-electron modes stay purely local. Finite-beam modes initialize IPPL, build a temporary particle container, and reduce histogram counts across MPI ranks before writing the normalized CSV output.
